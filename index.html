The Problem

English-first AI is
not India-first AI

🗣️

Language Exclusion at Scale

Most voice AI systems understand English well but fail catastrophically with Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, and other Indian languages — especially with dialectal variations and accents that differ by district.

🌐

Code-Mixed Speech Reality

Indians naturally switch between languages mid-sentence — Hinglish, Tanglish, Kanglish. Existing systems treat this as noise or error, when it's actually the primary way hundreds of millions communicate.

🏥

Critical Services Gated

Banking, healthcare, and government services remain inaccessible to rural users. A farmer in Bihar or a fisherman in Tamil Nadu cannot access digital services designed for English speakers.

📈

Billion-Dollar Opportunity Missed

India's next 500 million internet users will onboard via voice, not typing. The $40B voice commerce market is unreachable without language-inclusive infrastructure.
